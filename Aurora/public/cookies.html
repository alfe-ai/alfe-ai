Cookie & Session Usage

Last updated: 02/23/2026

We store a unique session ID in sessionStorage so your chat history and preferences stay associated with your current visit.

Your theme settings and feature flags are saved to localStorage along with whether you've accepted this notice.

These cookies and session analytics may be used for internal analysis (including product and marketing A/B testing), and are never shared with third parties for marketing analytics.
